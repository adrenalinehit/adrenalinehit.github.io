Http Codes - Privacy Policy

From and including version 1.7

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect app performance data and information about crashes.

The app does not collect any data that can identify you. I collect anonymous statistics about the favourites that you’re adding/removing and the memes that you’re sharing. In both instances just the status code and meme type.

Up to and including version 1.6

I do not collect any data via this app. (For example, I have no access to the favourites that you select.)

All versions

You may be sending diagnostic information to Apple if you have this enabled on your device.
